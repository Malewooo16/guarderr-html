Who Are We?

BusyBody Security is a vibrant, mid-sized company established to meet the growing demands for security, surveillance, bodyguard services, fire and rescue operations, and coordination with the police on criminal matters.
Our dedicated internal team boasts extensive experience in protecting people and property, fire and rescue services, and placement of front-end staff, including comprehensive training programs. This expertise is enhanced by our external consultants, who bring long-standing backgrounds in security, first aid, paramedical services, fire and rescue, and law enforcement.
